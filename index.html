Aircraft Technical Consulting International (airTCI) provides complete professional aviation technical expertise and support for aircraft, engine, and components with a team of experienced consultants who have extensive backgrounds in the airline operations and maintenance industry, and aircraft leasing with a focus on supporting sales and purchases, records management, and regulatory compliance. airTCI has collaborative agreements in place to support worldwide operations in a multi-lingual environment. airTCI works with the most experienced global consultant base to ensure support for our customers internationally.

Our customers range from owner/operators to lessors and lenders. Each client may have different requirements for which we provide expert custom consultation.

Experience does matter and we can customize our service to meet and exceed the requirements of our customers. When problems arise, airTCI is focused on solutions to issues.

Services

airTCI offers trusted, professional, and customer-oriented services that can be difficult to procure in the aviation industry. Services we offer include but are not limited to:

  • Program & Project Management
  • Records Management & Audits
  • Lease, Purchase & Sale Agreement Review
  • Vender Contract Agreement Review
  • Mid-term Lease Audits
  • Qualification Audits
  • MRO Representation
  • Regulatory Liaison
  • Delivery & Return
  • Technical Document Review
  • Physical Inspections of Airframes, Engines & Shops
  • Pass-thru DAR & Flight Ops
