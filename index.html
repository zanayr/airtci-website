airTCI provides complete professional aviation technical expertise for aircraft, engine andcomponents with a team of experienced consultants with extensive backgrounds and experience in aircraft leasing, airline operations, maintenance, sales and purchase, records management and regulatory compliance among others. Collaborative agreements are also in place to support worldwide operations in a multi-lingual environment. airTCI collaborates with the most experienced global consultant base to ensure support for our customers internationally.

Our customers range from owners/operators to lessors and lenders. Each customer may have different requirements and demands where we can provide consultation and expertise.

Experience does matter, and we can customize our service to exceed the requirements of our customers. There are many companies and individuals that offer these services, but we specialize and focus on offering solutions to the issues not just addressing them.
